Finance Books

Rich dad Poor dad should be the first book you read If you have a question like ” where to learn personal finance? ‘ It explains all the basics of finance and help you to develop a different thought process about money. Out of all books this was the bible for many people

All your worth is one of the best personal finance book which teaches you on budgeting and managing money, if you are really looking some valuable insight on managing money and improve your budgeting, you should read this book.

Think And Grow Rich is a good self book which is suitable for every one , it does not only a finance book but a self help book which will rewire your thought process. It helps you to figure out the purpose of your life.

Investonomy by Pranjal Kamra is a famous you tuber and investor, you can read this to understand all the basic fundamentals on stock market. Every beginner will enjoy reading this book which explains all the stock market terms.

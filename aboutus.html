About The Mango People

Hello All, Welcome to The Mango People !!!

My name is Lijo Francis, a common man talking about basic finance to the masses. I started The Mango People to educate people about money. Explaining finance in a simple way possible and discussing topics that come to my attention.

I believe financial education is more sidelined in our life and in our school educational system. It is taught to us at a later stage of life.

It is believed that it is not easy to understand money, but I found it was not true when I tried to become financially independent. My first insight about money and how it is sidelined from every individual came after reading this book called ” The Rich Dad And Poor Dad “. If you have come across this book, very good if not, I strongly recommend you to read it. Over the years of my journey to be financially independent, had made me acquire a few financial skills and knowledge which I am publishing via my blogs.

So what you will find on this blog ???

  • Give you meaningful insights about savings and investments.
  • Financial Services
  • In-depth information to make decisions involving money.
  • And tricks to save some money and still enjoy the luxury.
